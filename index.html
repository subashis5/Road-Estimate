About Smart Road Estimator

Revolutionizing road safety infrastructure planning with data-driven cost estimation

This innovative web application helps engineers, urban planners, and government agencies estimate road safety intervention costs quickly and accurately. Built for the National Road Safety Hackathon 2025, it combines sustainability with practicality to create safer road infrastructure.
